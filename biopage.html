Book 2: The Orb of Reoccurrence
Now Available!

When I was 14 years old, I was working at the town Nuclear Power Plant when I felt a pinching sensation on my neck. I frantically smacked at it, and when I pulled my hand back I saw green glowing spider guts. You can imagine what came next. Ever since then I've lived as a vigilante in Japan, fighting off creepos and baking giant cookies. I decided to jot down a few of my memoirs, and it reached a larger audience than I expected.
